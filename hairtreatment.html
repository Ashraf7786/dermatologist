Signs of Hair Problems You Might Experience

  • Excessive hair fall or shedding.
  • Thinning hair on the scalp or crown.
  • Dry, brittle, or weak hair strands.
  • Scalp issues like dandruff, itching, or inflammation.
  • Receding hairline or bald patches.
  • Loss of shine or unhealthy-looking hair.
