About us

Our Doctor

Dr. Imran Khan

Dr. Imran is a highly experienced and renowned dermatologist based in Rajasthan. He is widely recognized for his exceptional skills and expertise in clinical dermatology, dermasurgery, and aesthetic procedures such as chemical peeling, BOTOX, threadlift, fillers, and advanced laser treatments. After completing his medical degree (MBBS) and post-graduation (MD) from prestigious institutions, Dr. Imran gained extensive experience at a leading medical center where he honed his skills in a wide range of dermatological procedures. A visionary and pioneer in his field, Dr. Imran has been at the forefront of introducing new technologies to the state of Rajasthan. He is deeply committed to providing the best possible care to his patients, combining his exceptional skills, vast expertise, and a dedication to staying current with the latest advancements. His commitment to excellence makes him a highly respected and sought-after dermatologist.
