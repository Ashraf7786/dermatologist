Pigmentation issues may include dark spots, uneven skin tone, melasma, or sun-induced discoloration. You should see a doctor if spots persist, worsen, or affect your confidence. Certain factors can trigger or worsen pigmentation, including sun exposure, hormonal changes, stress, poor skincare habits, or genetics. At Dr. Imran Khan's Clinic, we offer personalized Pigmentation Treatment Solutions to lighten dark spots, balance skin tone, and restore clear, glowing skin effectively.

Signs of Pigmentation You Might Experience

  • Dark spots or patches on the face, neck, or hands.
  • Uneven skin tone (patchy or blotchy appearance).
  • Sunspots or age spots caused by sun exposure.
  • Melasma (brownish patches on cheeks, forehead, or upper lip).
  • Post-inflammatory marks (from acne or injuries).
  • Dull, tired-looking skin with discoloration.

Pigmentation Myths: What Doesn't Cause Dark Spots

Common myths include believing makeup or frequent facial expressions cause pigmentation. Pigmentation is caused by excess melanin production, not daily expressions.
