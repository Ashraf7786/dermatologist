Eczema symptoms may include red, itchy, or inflamed skin, dry patches, or recurrent flare-ups. You should see a doctor if symptoms are persistent or worsening. Certain factors can trigger or worsen eczema, including allergens, stress, harsh skincare, climate changes, or irritants. At Dr. Imran Khan's Clinic, we offer personalized Eczema Treatment Solutions to calm flare-ups, restore skin barrier function, and improve skin health effectively.

Signs of Eczema You Might Experience

  • Red, itchy patches on the skin (hands, arms, face).
  • Dry, scaly skin that may crack or bleed.
  • Inflammation and swelling of affected areas.
  • Recurrent flare-ups triggered by stress or irritants.
  • Thickened skin due to chronic scratching.
  • Irritation or discomfort that affects daily life.

Eczema Myths: What Doesn't Cause Flare-Ups

Common myths include believing diet alone or bathing habits cause eczema. Eczema is a complex condition influenced by genetics, immune response, and environmental triggers.
